Understanding Our Hair Transplant Techniques

At Redlineesthetic, we specialize in two primary hair transplant techniques:

  1. FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction): This minimally invasive technique involves extracting individual hair follicles from the donor area and implanting them into the recipient area. FUE is ideal for larger areas and offers quick recovery with minimal scarring.
  2. DHI (Direct Hair Implantation): An advanced form of FUE, DHI uses a special tool to extract and implant hair follicles in one step. This technique allows for more precise placement and is excellent for creating natural hairlines and working in smaller areas.

Our expert surgeons will recommend the best technique for your individual needs during your consultation, ensuring optimal results tailored to your unique situation.
